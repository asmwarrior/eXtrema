Colors

There are two groups of colors:

  • a standard set of fixed, named colors that are always defined.
    Named colors use index numbers starting at 0 and decreasing to -19, since there are 20 named colors.
  • a dynamically loaded colormap that can be changed at will.
    Dynamically loaded colors use color indexes starting at 1 and increasing to the number of colors in the colormap. See COLORMAP and COLORMAPFILE.
