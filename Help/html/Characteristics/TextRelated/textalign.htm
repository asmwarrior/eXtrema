TEXTALIGN

Syntax: SET TEXTALIGN n
GET TEXTALIGN { n }
Default value: TEXTALIGN = 1

TEXTALIGN controls the justification of the character strings that are drawn when using the TEXT command. The origin of the text string is always the lower left corner of the string. The justification determines where this origin is placed with respect to a reference point.

valuejustification
1   lower left
2   lower centre
3   lower right
4   centre left
5   centre centre
6   centre right
7   upper left
8   upper centre
9   upper right
10   upper left at 90 degrees
11   lower left at 270 degrees
12   lower centre at 90 degrees
