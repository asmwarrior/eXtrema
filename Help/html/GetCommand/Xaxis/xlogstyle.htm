XLOGSTYLE

Syntax: GET XLOGSTYLE {n}
Default value: XLOGSTYLE = 1

XLOGSTYLE controls whether or not the numbers labeling the large tic marks on the x-axis are displayed as powers of XLOGBASE when XLOGBASE > 1.0.

XLOGSTYLE = 0 → display the numbers in decimal form
XLOGSTYLE ≠ 0 → display the numbers in exponential form
